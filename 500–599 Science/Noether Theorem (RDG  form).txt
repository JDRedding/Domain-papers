RDG Noether Theorem
-------------------
Given a SID-automorphism δq = ε K and time shift δt = ε ξ:

Action:
    S[q] = ∫ L(q, D_t q, t) dt

Variation:
    δS = ε ∫ D_t[ (∂_{dot q} L) K  -  ξ L ] dt

Define RDG Noether Charge:
    J_RDG := (∂_{dot q} L) K  -  ξ L

Symmetry Condition:
    δS = 0  ⇒  D_t(J_RDG) = 0

Interpretation:
    SID symmetry → PED invariant
    Q-layer: [J_RDG, H] = 0
