GLOSSARY

SID - Structure / Interaction / Dynamics
    The conformal sector of RDG.  
    Scale‑free relational geometry derived from null propagation.  
    Provides conformal structure, gauge interactions, and null dynamics.  

PED - Projective / Evaluation / Dynamics
    The projective sector of RDG.  
    Scale‑bearing geometry derived from timelike geodesics and mass.  
    Provides projective structure, proper‑time evaluation, and timelike dynamics.  

Γ_renorm - Scale‑Bridging Operator
    The RDG renormalization operator that activates PED from SID.  
    Represents the SID→SID⊕PED transition.  
    Physically triggered by Higgs_VEV in standard cosmology.  

Q‑box - Moderation Layer
    The RDG moderation layer coupling SID and PED.  
    Governs how projective structure emerges from conformal structure.  

COSM  - Conformal‑Only Spacetime Module
    RDG module where only SID is active and PED is suppressed or absent.  
    Conformal geometry only; no metric; no proper time.  
    RDG_COSM^Bradonjić is the massless spacetime case.

RDG_COSM^substrate
    COSM regime where substrate dynamics generate PED early.  
    Conformal ⊕ projective geometry exists pre‑EWSB.  
    Big Bang singularity loses geometric justification.

RDG_spacetime^massive
    Post‑EWSB RDG regime where SID⊕PED is fully active.  
    Full metric geometry; proper time; GR valid.

EPS - Ehlers–Pirani–Schild Framework
    Geometric reconstruction from physical worldlines.  
    Null rays → conformal structure.  
    Massive worldlines → projective structure.  
    EPS_full = conformal ⊕ projective.

EPS_partial
    Only conformal structure present (null rays).  
    No projective structure (no massive worldlines).  
    Geometry is conformal‑only.

EWSB - Electroweak Symmetry Breaking
    Higgs_VEV turns on at ~246 GeV.  
    Mass appears; timelike geodesics appear; PED activates.

NullLayer
    Conformal‑only relational layer (SID-only).  
    No proper time; no timelike direction.

Projective Structure
    Geometry defined by massive worldlines and affine parameterization.  
    Requires PED.

Metric Geometry
    Full pseudo‑Riemannian geometry reconstructed from EPS_full.  
    Requires both SID and PED.
